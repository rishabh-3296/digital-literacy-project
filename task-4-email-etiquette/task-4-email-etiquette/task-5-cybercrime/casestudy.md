## Phishing Case Study

Phishing is a type of cybercrime where attackers trick users into sharing sensitive information like passwords or bank details.

For example, a student may receive a fake message that looks like it is from a bank or UPI app. The message asks the user to click a link and enter their details. Once entered, the attacker steals the information.

Students are often targeted because they may not be fully aware of such scams. The consequences include loss of money and account hacking.

This shows the importance of being careful while clicking links and sharing personal information online.

## Stay Safe Online

- Do not click unknown links
- Always verify website URLs
- Use strong passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share OTP with anyone
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for payments
- Check sender email carefully
- Use official apps for transactions

### UPI Safety
- Never share UPI PIN
- Do not accept unknown payment requests

Report cybercrime: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
